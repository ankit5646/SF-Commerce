S&F Commerce Academy and Consultancy in Greater Noida is the ideal place for developing requisite skills and acquiring industry-demanded training. It enables students and employees to build strong organizations and become great leaders who not only add value to their respective fields but also create lasting impressions on society. The Academy caters to coaching and training, skill building, research, and consultancy related to the diverse needs of commerce and business.
From coaching students of class XI/XII/CA/MBA/BCOM/BBA/MCOM to training and skill building for executives and managers, the Academy also provides necessary expertise to run businesses. In more than half a decade, the Academy has created a name for itself in Greater Noida, Noida, as well as in Delhi and the NCR area. Additionally, it specializes in niche areas such as providing consultancy in the field of industry-academia engagement, helping institutions and colleges secure good placements for students in reputed companies, and building industry networks for conducting MDP and training programs on campuses.
It has been bridging the gap between academia and industry by creating business models that have proven to be win-win situations for both.
