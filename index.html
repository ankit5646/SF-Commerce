S&F Commerce Academy and Consultancy at Greater Noida, is the ideal place of developing requisite skill and acquiring industry demanded training, to enable students and employees to build strong organizations and to become great leaders who not only add values to their respective fields but also creates lasting impression for the society. The Academy is catering to coaching and training, skill building, research and consultancy relating to diverse needs of commerce and business. From coaching to students of class XI/XII/CA/MBA/BCOM/BBA/MCOM to training and skill building to executives and managers, also providing necessary expertise to run business etc. In the more than half a decade, the Academy has created a name of its own in the places of Greater Noida, Noida, also in Delhi and NCR area. Further few niche areas such as providing consultancy in the field of industry-academia engagement, helping institution and colleges to get good placement of students in reputed companies, industry network for conducting MDP and training programs by campuses. It has been bridging the gap between Academia and Industry, by creating business models which has proved to be win-win situation for both.
